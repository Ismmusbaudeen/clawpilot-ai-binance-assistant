# ClawPilot AI — Binance Smart Trading Co-Pilot

ClawPilot AI is an intelligent assistant designed to enhance the trading experience on Binance.  
It provides real-time trade insights, portfolio analysis, risk detection, and AI-powered guidance to help users make smarter crypto trading decisions.

This project demonstrates how an AI assistant can simplify trading by analyzing market signals, portfolio exposure, and sentiment data.

---

## 🚀 Features

### AI Trade Insights
Provides real-time trading insights based on market conditions.

- Entry and exit zone suggestions
- Stop-loss and take-profit recommendations
- Trade risk analysis
- Technical indicator explanations

---

### Portfolio Health Score
Analyzes the user portfolio and calculates a **portfolio health score**.

- Asset allocation analysis
- Diversification insights
- Volatility exposure detection
- Risk concentration alerts

---

### AI Market Briefing
Generates quick summaries of important crypto market events.

- Trending tokens
- Whale activity alerts
- Sentiment analysis
- Major crypto news summaries

---

### Risk & Scam Protection
Helps users avoid risky trades and suspicious tokens.

- Pump-and-dump detection
- High volatility alerts
- Liquidation risk warnings
- Suspicious token detection

---

### AI Crypto Tutor
An educational assistant that explains crypto and trading concepts.

Example questions:

- What is RSI?
- Explain this chart.
- Why is BTC going up?

The assistant gives simple explanations directly inside the trading interface.

---

## 🧠 How It Works

ClawPilot AI combines several AI components:

- Market data analysis
- Sentiment analysis from crypto news and social platforms
- Portfolio risk analysis models
- Natural language AI assistant

The system processes these signals and generates **clear, actionable insights** for the user.
